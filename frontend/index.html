Everything You Need To Know

Common questions about Amul Stock Notifier.

Is Amul Stock Notifier free?

Yes. The service is completely free to use.

Do I need Telegram?

Yes. Telegram is used to instantly notify you when your selected products become available.

Which products can I track?

All currently supported Amul High Protein products available on the official Amul website.

How does pincode tracking work?

Stock availability can vary by delivery location. Your selected pincode ensures alerts are relevant to your area.

Is this an official Amul product?

No. This is an independent stock tracking tool created for the community and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Amul.
